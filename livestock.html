Livestock Farming

Livestock farming involves the raising of domesticated animals for meat, dairy, fiber, and other products.

To be successful in livestock farming, you will need:

  • Animals (such as cows, pigs, sheep, goats, or horses)
  • Feed and water
  • Shelter (such as barns or sheds)
  • Equipment (such as milking machines or shearing tools)
  • Health management (such as vaccination and disease control)
