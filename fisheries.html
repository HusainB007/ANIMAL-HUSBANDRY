Fisheries

Fisheries involve the farming, catching, and processing of fish and other aquatic organisms for food, sport, and other purposes.

To be successful in fisheries, you will need:

  • Water bodies (such as ponds, lakes, and rivers)
  • Fish or other aquatic organisms
  • Feed and water quality management
  • Fishing equipment (such as nets and traps)
  • Processing equipment (such as gutting knives and smoking ovens)
