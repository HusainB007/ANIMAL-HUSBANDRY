Dairy Farming

Dairy farming is one of the most important sectors of animal husbandry in India. It involves the production of milk, cheese, butter, and other dairy products.

To be successful in dairy farming, you will need:

  • Cows or buffaloes
  • Feed and fodder (such as grass, hay, and silage)
  • Water
  • Farm equipment (such as milking machines and cooling tanks)
